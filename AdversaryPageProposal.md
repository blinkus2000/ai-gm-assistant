adversary adventure tool proposal

4 types of adventures

THWARTING - PC's vs BBEG
COLLECTING -  PC's get something
DELIVERING - PC's transfer stuff
DISCOVERY - PC's find something

4 types of adversaries

HEAVY HITTERS -  tough can lay a huge beatdown
RACERS - they are racing after something the PC's must get to it first. 
CHASERS -  the PC's have something it wants
SHADOW - manipulator sneaky


HEAVY HITTER (HH):
1. Intro HH to PC'
2. PC's learn about HH
3. PC's learn HH has a big goal
4. PC's battle HH minions
5. PC's battle HH

RACER (RC):
1. PC's learn of the race.
2. PC's slowed by RC/NPC's
3. PC's learn about the RC
4. PC's must reach the goal
5. PC's prevent/beat the RC

CHASER (CC):
1. PC's gain a valuable thing
2. PC's escape from CC
3. PC's learn why CC wants thing
4. PC's must reach goal
5. PC's defeat CC goal

SHADOW (SH):
1. PC's have no idea of SH (something is not right)
2. PC's discover bigger plot
3. PC's learn of SH
4. PC's track down SH
5. PC's defeat SH
